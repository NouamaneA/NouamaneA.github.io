Nouamane Arhachoui

I’m a PhD Student in Computer Science at the LIP6 lab - Sorbonne Université. My work focuses on developing graph algorithms and machine learning models that incorporate users’ interactions to the network structure of social platforms to detect opinion groups and influence propagation. I’m a member of the Complex Networks research team at the LIP6 and I’m affiliated with the Networks and Services department of Télécom SudParis, Institut Polytechnique de Paris.

07/2022 – Maintained

PsiScore

A python package for social graph analysis with the Psi-score metric.

This Python library provides several implementations of the PsiScore, an influence metric that generalizes the PageRank in the context of social networks.
Provided methods
  • - Power-Psi and Power-NF, two algorithms based on power iterations
  • - Push-Psi and Push-NF, two algorithms based on a push-flow algorithm.
